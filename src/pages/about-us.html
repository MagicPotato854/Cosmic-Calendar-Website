About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of students around the Denver area schools that met at Warren Tech to pursue a greater understanding of computers and engineering. Together, we all decided to join HUNCH as a way to show what we can do, even if some of us may be new to all of the software and new languages.

Our Mission

Our mission is to innovate and develop a seamless, accurate system for translating Earth-based time into planetary calendars, empowering future interplanetary exploration and communication by creating an efficient platform for communication.
